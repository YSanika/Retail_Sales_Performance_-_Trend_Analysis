# 📌 Project Overview

Retail organizations generate vast amounts of transactional data every day. Transforming this data into meaningful business insights is essential for improving sales performance, optimizing operations, and supporting strategic decision-making.

This project performs an end-to-end retail sales analysis using Python to evaluate revenue trends, profitability, customer segments, regional performance, and key business metrics through exploratory data analysis (EDA) and visualization.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

The analysis aims to:

- Analyze overall sales performance
- Measure revenue and profit growth
- Identify top-performing product categories
- Evaluate regional sales performance
- Understand customer segment contribution
- Detect seasonal sales trends
- Support data-driven business decisions

---

# 💡 Key Business Insights

- Revenue demonstrates consistent long-term growth.
- Technology products generate the highest sales.
- West region contributes the largest share of revenue.
- Consumer customers represent the highest revenue contribution.
- Revenue is diversified across products, reducing business risk.
- Seasonal demand patterns are visible throughout the year.
